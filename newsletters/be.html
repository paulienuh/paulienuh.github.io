Built Environment Networking Event
1st March, 1pm - 4pm

Ron Johnston Room & USP Cafe

Geography & Planning Building
This event aims to give employers in the Built Environment industry an opportunity to make contact with students from relevant disciplines. This includes students from Civil Engineering, Urban Studies and Planning, Real Estate, Urban Design, Cities and Global Development, Advanced GIS and Geography. 

There will be an opportunity to network with people directly in the industry. Most of the employers have various placements, work experience and graduate opportunities.
