National Careers Week
with USP

Running from 6th - 10th March 2017, NCW is a not-for-profit company to promote the importance of good careers education in schools and colleges. Here at USP, we are dedicated to enhancing student's employability with the engagement of alumni and external employers in the industry and increasing awareness of careers and resources available.

For the week, we will be tweeting opportunities related to careers with your degree, events and workshops you can take part in. Here's an overview of the themes for the week:
