This event is free to attend and open to all. Register your place.

About Lynsey

Lynsey is the author of ESTATES: AN INTIMATE HISTORY, and RESPECTABLE: THE EXPERIENCE OF CLASS, and visiting fellow in cultural studies at Liverpool John Moores University. She is a regular contributor to the Guardian, and has also written for The Observer, Daily Telegraph, Sunday Times, New Statesman, Prospect, and Times Literary Supplement.
