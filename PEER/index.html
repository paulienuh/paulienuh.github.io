Placements
Well done to our third-year students who have now completed their Easter placements. According to the feedback from employers, many of our students were professional and very engaged.

We hope that the experience has been valuable. Don't let this be your final placement, keep in contact with your employer and continue searching for hands-on experience to stand out from the crowd when you graduate.

"Placements are an amazing way to get involved in planning in practice and explore the different career routes of a Planning degree prior to graduation. 

This is a fantastic opportunity that CBRE have offered me and I can't wait to get started."

- Katie Milnes
Katie Milnes who is on the MPlan Urban Studies and Planning successfully secured another placement with CBRE over the summer - adding further experience to her CV!
